Grilled Steak Salad

A healthy way to eat your steak!

Ingredients:

  • 1 3/4 pounds beef sirloin steak
  • 1/3 cup olive oil
  • 3 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 3/4 cup crumbled blue cheese
  • 8 cups romaine lettuce - rinsed, dried, and torn into bit-size pieces
  • 1 small green bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 carrot, sliced
  • 1/2 cup sliced red onion

Directions:

  1. Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat and lightly oil the grate.
  2. Place steak on preheated grill and cook for 3 to 5 minutes per side or until desired doneness is reached. Remove from heat and let sit until cool enough to handle. Slice steak into strips or bite-sized pieces.
  3. Whisk olive oil, vinegar, lemon juice, Worcestershire sauce, garlic, salt, and pepper together in a small bowl; stir in blue cheese. Cover and place dressing in refrigerator.
  4. Arrange lettuce, tomato, pepper, onion, and olives onto chilled plates. Top with steak and drizzle with dressing.
